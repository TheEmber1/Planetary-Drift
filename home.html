How to Play Planetary Drift

1

Place the Planet

First, click anywhere to strategically position the planet on each level. This will serve as your gravitational anchor.

2

Launch Your Spaceship

Click anywhere to spawn your spaceship, then drag to aim and release to launch. The longer you drag, the more power you'll get.

3

Collect Orbs

Navigate through space to collect the glowing orbs. Use the planet's gravity to help slingshot your spaceship around obstacles.

4

Mind Your Bounces

You have a limited number of bounces off the planet. Don't exceed your limit or the game will end!

Controls

  • Left Click: Place planet, spawn spaceship, aim and launch
  • Right Click: Cancel aim and reposition spaceship
  • ESC: Restart current level
